Biography

Seonmyeong Bak is a Ph.D candidate of Computer Science at Georgia Institute of Technology. His research interests include high performance computing, programming models and runtime systems and scheduling issues in task-level parallel programming models. He started his Ph.D in University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign(Advisor: Prof. Laxmikant Kale) and transferred to Georgia Institute of Technology as a Ph.D candidate(Qualifier, Coursework waived) after his first 3 years.(Advisor: Prof. Vivek Sarkar)
